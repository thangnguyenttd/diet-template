Corporate Programs

Boring nutrition lectures are a thing of the past. People working in corporate America sit in front of their computers and look at statistics, graphs and numbers all day long. The last thing they want to see is a health presentation with more graphs and numbers.

Integrative Nutritionist Inna Topiler is a polished, inspiring, humorous and engaging presenter. Your audience will be informed and delighted, and come away feeling energized about making positive and powerful changes in their health. Inna has a variety of lectures, seminars and group sessions.

Topics include :

  • General Nutrition (healthy eating habits, healthy shopping, reading labels, healthy cooking etc)
  • Sugar Blues
  • Coffee addictions
  • Emotional Eating
  • Carbs, the good, the bad and the ugly
  • Digestive Wellness
  • Feeding our Children
  • We can also customize a lecture on any nutritional topic of your choice
